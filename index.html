Scouts BSA Troop 19 for Girls Serving lower Westchester county

We're a Scouts BSA troop of girls serving Tuckahoe Village and surrounding communities. The three aims of Scouting are: character development, citizenship training, and personal fitness.

We accomplish this through camping, outdoor activities, and community service—and we have a lot of fun while we're at it! Our troop is truly youth-led. We follow the patrol method, so the scouts set the goals, agenda, and activities—and then make it all happen with the help of adult leaders.

Join us at a troop meeting to see what scouting is all about. We meet Thursdays, 7:00 to 8:30 PM, at the Tuckahoe Community Center.
